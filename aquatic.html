Aquatic Plants

Having a pond in your house it can be satisfying because it reconnects you more to nature. If you are one of the lucky ones to have a pond in their house, you are in the right place! In the Fig Tree plant store, we sell good quality aquatic plants for your ponds that will make them look even more stunning.

Featured Products

Jumbo-Jurassic Water Lettuce

Jumbo-Jurassic Water Lettuce are large, green, tropical plants that float on the surface of the water. These can grow even wider and taller (10 - 14 inches) if you keep them crowded. No planting is necessary, simply place these floating plants directly on the surface of the water. This is the largest water lettuce that we sell. Jumbo-Jurassic Water Lettuce adds shade immediately, giving fish protection from the sun. Jurassic Water Lettuce increases uptake of nutrients from the pond water, helping to eliminate algae. Great in ponds and container gardens. An excellent choice as a pond filter as well!


Price: $3.99


Water Hyacinth

Water Hyacinths are lovely, emerald-green, aquatic plants with bulbous foliage and pretty lavender blooms, one of the loveliest and one of the most popular of all floating water plants! A true floating plant with roots that hang down in the water, helping to purify and clean the water and also shading the water and helping with algae elimination. Shading the water helps to reduce algae growth and adds cover for fish. When Water Hyacinths take up nutrients from the water, it starves out the algae.


Price: $8.99


Giant Papyrus

Giant Papyrus, also known as Egyptian Papyrus is a tropical plant with an exotic look and brings a touch of the tropics to your backyard, water garden or patio. Giant Papyrus grows very quickly with large, shaggy green pom poms or plant spikelets, as its foliage. This tall specimen adds a dramatic touch to your water garden feature! Great next to your pond or pool side. Giant Papyrus works well in terrestrial gardens too!


Price: $18.99


Blue Flag Iris

Blue Flag Iris or Iris Versicolor has lovely, deep, bluish-purple blooms on this attractive iris with blue-green, sword-like foliage. Foliage stays nice all summer long on Blue Flag Iris and it adds a nice pop of color to your spring water garden, growing 24 - 30 inches tall. Blue Flag Iris blooms early or late spring, depending on the zone. Blue Flag Iris is one of the most popular iris varieties!


Price: $19.99
