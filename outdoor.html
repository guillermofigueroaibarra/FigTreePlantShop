Outdoor Plants

If you would like to have a beautiful front yard or backyard, you can choose from our beautiful outdoor plants. The Fig Tree plant store has a variety of trees, pines, and other plants that will make your house have a fascinating look. Most of these outdoor plants need lots of space and sunlight to grow healthy, that's why they need to be outside your house.

Featured Products

Fluffy Western Arborvitae

The name says it all. Fluffy Western arborvitae is an ideal package of texture and color. This semi-dwarf cultivar naturally grows as a pyramid of very glossy bright yellow foliage that results in an appealing, voluminous texture in the landscape. Hardy and burn resistant, it maintains color even through winter. More deer resistant than T. occidentalis.


Price: $24.99


Polar Gold

Bright year-round, Polar Gold arborvitae has attractive gold evergreen foliage. Similar to 'Brandon' in size and habit, with dense, finely textured foliage that is resistant to windburn. A useful size for residential landscapes.


Price: $24.99


Sunjoy Gold Beret Barberry

Sunjoy Gold Beret barberry is a super-small mounding type with bright gold foliage, attractive red new growth, and red fall color. Proven to be burn resistant in side by side tests with other yellow leaf barberry. A low maintenance plant for mass plantings or tight spaces that need a shot of bright color. Of particular interest to those with rock gardens, outdoor railroads, and dwarf conifer collections.


Price: $16


Katsura Japanese Maple tree

The embodiement of elegance. We're pleased to offer own-root liners of select Japanese maple varieties. 'Katsura' is a classic tree-form type, with small, long-lobed leaves. Foliage emerges bronze tinged with red, matures to lime green, then blazes bright orange and red in autumn. Forms a small, graceful tree, with appealing branching.


Price: $39.99
